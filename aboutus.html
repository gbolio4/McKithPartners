About McKith Partners


McKith Partners is an organization development, change management and search consulting firm. We are Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC)-registered and Chartered Institute of Personnel Management of Nigeria (CIPM)-licensed to provide business advisory and HR consultancy services to our numerous clients in the Mini, Small & Medium Scale Enterprises (MSME) sector of the Nigerian economy. Our firm is modeled after international best practice of Strategic Business Partnership.


We partner with our clients for excellence, success and long-term sustainability. We are driven by the passion to ensure that our clients meet their short, medium and long-term people priorities and are committed to supporting clients in aligning their HR strategy with overall business vision by developing strategic HR priorities that fit into their job ticket.

Our vision is to provide turnkey solutions that make long-term impact on the health and wellbeing of clients’ business, while ultimately increasing efficiencies, profits and high returns on shareholders’ investment.
