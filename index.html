Welcome to the World of McKith Partners

MCKITH PARTNERS is an organization development, change management and search consulting firm. We are Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC)-registered and Chartered Institute of Personnel Management of Nigeria (CIPM)-licensed to provide business advisory services to our numerous clients in the Mini, Small & Medium Scale Enterprises (MSME) sector of the Nigerian economy.

At McKITH PARTNERS, our firm is modeled after international best practice of Strategic Business Partnership.
